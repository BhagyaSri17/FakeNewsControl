Control Mechanism for Managing Information Spread in Online Social Networks

Welcome

Online social networks (OSNs) enable users to share information quickly, but they also facilitate the spread of negative content, including misinformation. This can mislead users and impact society. Controlling negative information while allowing positive content to flow is a challenging task. Our system manages both positive and negative information in OSNs by analyzing user posts in real time. It reduces the spread of negative content using two strategies. The system also tracks user behavior over time and allows admins to monitor content trends, ensuring a balanced and reliable information flow.

Social Interactions

The Adaptive Control Mechanism for Managing Information Spread in Online Social Networks focuses on analyzing user-generated content to regulate the flow of positive and negative information. The system processes each post using advanced natural language processing (NLP) techniques to determine sentiment and detect potential misinformation. It incorporates user behavior tracking by maintaining a record of negative posts over time.
